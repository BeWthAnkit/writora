Effective Date: June 3, 2025

  •    Full name

  •    Other contact details voluntarily provided

       We may automatically collect certain technical data when you visit our Website, such as:

  •    Your IP address
  •    Browser type

  •    Date and time of access

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

7. Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Our Website is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.
